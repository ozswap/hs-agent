CHAPTER 45
CORK AND ARTICLES OF CORK
IX
45-1
Note
1. This chapter does not cover:
(a) Footwear or parts of footwear of chapter 64;
(b) Headgear or parts of headgear of chapter 65; or
(c) Articles of chapter 95 (for example, toys, games, sports equipment).
Additional U.S. Note
1. For the purposes of subheading 4504.10.20, the term "compressed cork" means forms molded under heat and compression
from cork particles without the addition of other materials.
