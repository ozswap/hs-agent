CHAPTER 81
OTHER BASE METALS; CERMETS; ARTICLES THEREOF
XV
81-1
Additional U.S. Notes
1. Alloys of each of the base metals provided for in chapter 81 are metals in which the content of the respective base metal is, by
weight, less than 99 percent, but not less than any other metallic element.
2. For the purposes of subheading 8108.90.60, the rate of duty "Free (C)" appearing in the "Special" subcolumn applies only to
tubes and pipes with attached fittings, suitable for conducting gases or liquids.
